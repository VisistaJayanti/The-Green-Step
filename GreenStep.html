About Us

An interactive mobile application

"The Green Step" is an interactive mobile application that promotes sustainability by accelerating walkability and use of public transport in UAE.

The primary function of this application is to track and monitor the step count of individuals on the walkable pathways. Subsequently, this step count will be converted into a specific monetary value. This money can be used to recharge the NOL cards, prompting and facilitating an easier utilization of public transport services.

This mobile application is capable of tracing the routes taken by pedestrians on the walkable pathways via GPS technology.

Main Aims

Healthy Ecosystems

Encouraging walking short distances not only promotes walking, the most sustainable mode of transport but also contributes to the enhancement of individual well-being and health by promoting physical fitness.

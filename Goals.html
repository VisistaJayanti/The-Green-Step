RTA and SDG Goals

"The Green Step" is an interactive mobile application that promotes sustainability by accelerating walkability and use of public transport in UAE.

Our application aligns with the RTA goals based on RTA sustainability report 2021 i.e, community engagement, health and safety, reducing carbon emissions, optimizing the use of energy. Our application issues social challenge of promoting walkability, promoting transport engagement, economic challenge of reducing cost and energy consumption and proposes economic benefits. It also addresses the enviornmental challenge of air pollution and global warming.

"The Green Step" is holistically aligned with SDG goals set by UN. The application follows SDG 3 - Good health and well-being, SDG 11- Sustainable Cities and Communities, SDG 13- Climate Action.
